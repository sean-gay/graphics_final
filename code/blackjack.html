Instructions

To start a game, simply press the "Start new Game" button. The game has two screens, one where you place your bet, and one where you can view the results of the current hand. Place a bet by clicking on the chips found on the screen to the left. Your personal betting info can be found right below these instructions. Please note that the maximum amount you can bet in one turn is $100 and the maximum number of chips which you can bet at one time is 5 chips. Good luck!
